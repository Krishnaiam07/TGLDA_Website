ABOUT

Telangana Livestock Development Agency (TGLDA) is an autonomous organization functioning under the Department of Animal Husbandry, Government of Telangana. The Agency came into existence on 2nd June 2014 following the formation of the State of Telangana after the bifurcation of the erstwhile Andhra Pradesh Livestock Development Agency (APLDA). TGLDA is committed to improving livestock productivity through scientific breeding programmes and modern reproductive technologies. The Agency provides quality artificial insemination services, produces and supplies frozen semen, promotes genetic improvement of cattle and buffaloes, and implements advanced breeding technologies such as In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) and Embryo Transfer Technology (ETT). Through its network of breeding centres, laboratories, and trained field personnel, TGLDA supports livestock farmers by improving animal productivity, enhancing milk production, conserving indigenous breeds, and contributing to the sustainable growth of Telangana's livestock and dairy sector.

Organizational Structure: Telangana Livestock Development Agency (TGLDA) functions through a well-defined administrative and technical framework to ensure the effective implementation of livestock development programmes across the State. The Agency is headed by the Chairman and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) at the State Headquarters, who oversee policy implementation, planning, and programme management. Field operations are coordinated through District Livestock Development Agencies (DLDAs) functioning in Adilabad, Warangal, Karimnagar, Khammam, Medak, Mahabubnagar, Nalgonda, Nizamabad, and Rangareddy. Each DLDA is supported by technical and administrative personnel, including Executive Officers, Deputy Directors, Veterinary Officers, and field staff, who work together to provide quality breeding services, artificial insemination, genetic improvement programmes, and farmer support across Telangana.

MISSION

To enhance livestock productivity and improve the livelihoods of farmers in Telangana by delivering quality breeding services, promoting scientific genetic improvement programmes, ensuring the availability of superior breeding inputs, adopting advanced reproductive technologies, conserving indigenous breeds, and providing efficient, farmer-centric livestock development services across the State.

VISION

To build a healthy, genetically superior, and productive livestock population in Telangana by delivering quality breeding services, ensuring doorstep access to modern reproductive technologies, improving animal health and milk production, empowering livestock farmers, and promoting sustainable livestock development for enhanced rural prosperity.
